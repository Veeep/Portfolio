About Me

I am a practitioner of Traditional Chinese Medicine that is currently practicing in Tumwater, WA.

I am the IT supervisor of annual botanical medicine conferences.

I am originally from southern Illinois, but have been in the glorious PNW since 2010.

I bought mt first home in southern Illinois in 2008, and taught myself how to completely renovate it into a rental property.

I am a huge nature lover and have always been apprehensive of having a career in tech since I didn't want to end up spending too much time in front of a computer inside.

But after seeing a modern coding open-office studio in Portland that had a beautiful view, plenty of sunshine and dogs running around happily, I realized that my aptitude for technology and interest with coding didn't mean I would have to be working in a dark cave to do my job!
